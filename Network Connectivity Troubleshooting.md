#### Network Connectivity Troubleshooting
---
1. Ping the edge router  
`ping 192,168.2.1`
2. Ping downstream to the Cisco switch  
`ping 10.0.0.2`
3. Ping Cisco switch Edge Router port  
`ping 10.0.0.1`
4. Ping our physical connection to Cisco switch  
`ping 10.0.30.1`
5. Ping PFSense  
`ping 172.16.30.1`
6. Ping the Sensor  
`ping 172.16.30.100`
